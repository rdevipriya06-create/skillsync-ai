📖 About SkillSync AI

What is SkillSync AI?

SkillSync AI is a free career-growth platform that uses artificial intelligence to analyze the gap between your current skills and your dream job. We give you a personalized roadmap, curated courses, and an AI career assistant — all in one place.

🚀 How to Use SkillSync AI — Step by Step

1
Create Your Free Account
Click "Get Started" on the home page. Enter your name, email and password — or sign up instantly with Google. It's completely free, no credit card needed.
2
Select Your Dream Job
On the Analysis page, type or select your dream job from 15 supported roles — Web Developer, Data Scientist, AI/ML Engineer, Cybersecurity Analyst, and more.
3
Enter Your Current Skills
Type each skill you already know and click "Add" (or press Enter). Add as many as you like — e.g. Python, HTML, Excel, SQL. Also choose how many hours per week you can study.
4
Get Your Skill Gap Analysis
Click "Analyze My Skills". Our AI instantly shows you a match score, which skills you already have, and which ones you need to learn to land that job.
5
Follow Your Learning Roadmap
Go to the Roadmap tab. You'll see a week-by-week plan with detailed daily steps — e.g. Week 1, Step 1: Introduction to HTML, Step 2: Setting up your editor, etc.
6
Browse Courses & Start Learning
Visit the Courses tab for hand-picked YouTube videos, freeCodeCamp courses, and more — specific to your job role and missing skills. Click "Start Learning" to open the resource directly.
7
Track Progress & Update Profile
Mark courses as "Skill Learned" when you finish them. Check your Progress page to see skill completion bars, and your Profile to view all your stats and goals at a glance.
8
Ask the AI Career Assistant
Click the 💬 chat button (bottom-right) anytime to ask our AI career assistant questions — "How long will it take to learn React?", "What projects should I build?", "How do I get my first job?" — it knows your profile!
